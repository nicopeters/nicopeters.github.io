üåçWhat is Berlin 2030?

We Berliners can make our city a carbon-neutral metropolis by 2030!
It's a legally binding referendum ("Volksbegehren", no petition). If it gets enough signatures and votes, the law changes. No ifs and buts!
Let's turn Berlin into a global lighthouse and help transform the world to zero emissions.
To make this happen, at least 180.000 signatures need to be collected on paper(!) between July and November 2022.
